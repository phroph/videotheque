How much does your service cost?

$10/month for the limited plan, and we also have 3 unlimited plans:
$15/month for 1 movie out at-a-time;
$20/month for 2 movies out at-a-time;
$25/month for 3 movies at-a-time.
For those who want to simply look-around, we provide free access to view our entire collection.

What is the difference between the limited and unlimited plan?

We here at VideoPile offer 4 kind of plans. 1 limited, and 3 unlimited. There are 4 types of plans, 1 limited plan and 3 unlimited plans. The limited plan allows one to view 1 movie at-a-time and at most 2 movies per month. The 3 unlimited plans allow one to view 1-3 movies at-a-time, respectively, and place no limit on how many movies you can view per month. The limited plan has the lowest monthly fee, whereas for the 3 unlimited plans, the more movies the user chooses to have access to at-a-time, the higher the monthly fee.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up by registering a new account with us, and choosing your plan during the registration process. Registration is free, and takes 2 minutes!

What is a movie queue?

A movie queue is a way of checking out movies you want to watch in the near future, if your watching the latest episode of Batman, and now want to see great adventures of AhmadMan, simply add AhmadMan to your queue, by clicking the 'add to queue' button, and it will be waiting for you.
